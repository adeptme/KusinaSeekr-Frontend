Pork Adobo

Pork Adobo is pork cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. It’s considered as the Philippines’ national dish because of its popularity.

40 mins 4 servings 4.8 (100 reviews)

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs and drumsticks
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Steamed white rice

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken in soy sauce, garlic, and peppercorns for 30 mins.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and brown the chicken on all sides.
  3. Add the reserved marinade, water, bay leaves, and brown sugar.
  4. Simmer for 30 minutes until tender and sauce thickens.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice.
