Basic Knife Skills

Master essential knife handling techniques that make food prep easier, faster, and safer. Learn how to properly hold a knife, practice chopping motions, and maintain your tools.

×

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Start with a stable cutting board and a sharp knife.
  2. Grip the knife with your thumb and index finger on the blade for control.
  3. Use a rocking motion when slicing vegetables.
  4. Keep your guiding hand in a “claw” shape to protect your fingers.
  5. Clean and dry your knife after every use.

Required Equipment

  • Chef's Knife
  • Cutting Board
  • Kitchen Towels
  • Vegetables or Ingredients for Practice
  • Sharpening Steel (optional)
